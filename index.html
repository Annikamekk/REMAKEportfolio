About me

Hey there! I’m Annika — a third-year Communication & Multimedia Design student at AUAS who’s all about front-end development with a soft spot for UI design. I love turning ideas into clean, interactive websites that not only work well but look great too.

Design and development are my main jam, but I’m also a big fan of storytelling through visuals. Last semester, I had the amazing opportunity to study in Vietnam, where I explored the world of photography and arts while diving deep into a totally new culture (and yes, I took way too many street photos). That experience continues to inspire how I approach design and creativity today. If you would like to know more about my time in vietnam at RMIT click here then!

When I’m not behind my laptop or camera, you’ll probably find me daydreaming about my next trip, volunteering with animals, or getting lost in a local market.
